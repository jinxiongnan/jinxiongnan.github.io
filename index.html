Xiongnan Jin (金雄男, 김웅남)

  • Hello! I am Xiongnan Jin, Assistant Professor at Shenzhen University (SZU).

  • Xiongnan Jin is an Assistant Professor at Shenzhen University. He received his Bachelor’s degree from Zhejiang University, and integrated Ph.D. degree from Yonsei University (supervised by Prof. Kyong-Ho Lee). Before joining Shenzhen University, he was a senior researcher at Zhejiang Lab and a post-doc researcher at National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST). His research interests lie on artificial intelligence topics, such as knowledge graphs, large models, multi-modal data, data mining, and natural language processing.
